Characters Description

Crisostomo Ibarra

  • He is a young, wise man who studied in Europe and then came back to the Philippines.
  • During the Spanish colonial era, he stands for the aspirations of the Filipino people for justice, equality, and freedom.

Maria Clara

  • She is a symbol of pureness, innocence, and a standard Filipina during their time.
  • Her father is Capitan Tiago.

Padre Damaso

  • He is portrayed as a Spanish Friar.
  • He is a abusive figure.

Sisa

  • Sisa is a mother of two children named Crispin and Basilio.
  • She is a caring and loving mother.

Capitan Tiago

  • He is the father of Maria Clara
  • He represents the Filipino elite or the upper class of the Spanish colonial period.
